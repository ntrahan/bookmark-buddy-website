Ever since I switched to Chrome as my primary browser many years ago, I'd been missing the ability to view my bookmarks in a sidebar. Since literally every other desktop browser ever made had one, I was sure Google would eventually follow suit and add this basic feature to their own browser, but alas, that day never came. I looked for extensions, but all the so-called sidebars out there actually hovered over the page you were looking at. "That's not a real sidebar!", I would cry out (in my head). It seemed I would have to take matters into my own hands if I wanted things to change, and so I set out on a mission to create a real sidebar for Chrome. After an embarrassingly large amount of time, I think I got pretty close, and Bookmark Buddy was born. Hope you enjoy.
-Nicolas, December 2021
